WHAT THIS DOES
--------------
Upload your procedures, manuals, or technical documents.
Ask a question in plain English.  You get:

  (1)  A short answer built ONLY from your documents
  (2)  An EVIDENCE panel — verbatim quotes, each one a click
       away from the exact highlighted passage in the source
  (3)  A straight "Not found in your documents." when your
       documents don't contain the answer.  No making things up.

Works with .pdf, .docx, .txt and .md files (15 MB each).


HOW TO USE IT  (3 STEPS)
------------------------
 STEP 1 — Double-click "Start Docs GPT.bat"
          A browser tab opens at http://127.0.0.1:8756
          (First time: needs Python 3.11+ installed — python.org)

 STEP 2 — Click the gear (⚙ Settings) and paste your Anthropic
          API key, then "Save on this PC".
          Get a key at:  console.anthropic.com
          You pay only for your own questions — typically a few
          cents each.

 STEP 3 — Ask a question, or try the built-in demo documents:
          "What are the acceptable gas test limits for confined
           space entry?"
          Then CLICK the citation — the source opens with the
          exact passage highlighted.  That's the whole point:
          never trust, always verify.


GOOD TO KNOW
------------
 * Your documents stay on YOUR computer.  When you ask a
   question, the document text + your question go to Anthropic
   (the Claude AI company) using YOUR key — nowhere else.
 * Never upload documents you are not allowed to share with a
   cloud AI service (confidential company files, personal data).
 * Scanned/photographed PDFs are not supported yet (no OCR).
 * The answer is a pointer — the document is the authority.
   Open the citation before acting on a number or a limit.
